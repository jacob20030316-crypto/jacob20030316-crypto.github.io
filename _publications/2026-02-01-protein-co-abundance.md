An LLM-agent pipeline for generating and comparing protein co-abundance networks across different human tissues. This work leverages large language model agents to automate the construction and analysis of tissue-specific protein interaction networks.

**Authors**: Runyu Guan, **Dehao Wu**, Qiqi Xie, Yang Li, Haohan Wang
