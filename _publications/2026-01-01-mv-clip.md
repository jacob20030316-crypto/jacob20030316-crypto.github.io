Developed MV-CLIP, a multi-view multimodal framework integrating text-only, image-only, and cross-modal interaction representations for sarcasm detection. Introduced gated bidirectional cross-attention and dual-path fusion with learnable dynamic weighting to enhance fine-grained text–image alignment. Improved Accuracy/Precision/F1 by ~3% over strong CLIP-based baselines while significantly reducing token usage and inference latency compared to LLM-based approaches.
