A contrastive cross-modal framework that projects DNA sequences and biological text into a shared latent space, overcoming LLMs' inability to effectively model long, non-linguistic genomic sequences. The learned gene-level embeddings enable grounded multimodal reasoning and improved performance on downstream biological prediction tasks.

**Authors**: **Dehao Wu**, Kowshika Sarker, Zixuan Sun, Chengxiang Zhai
