A global-to-local dynamic attention framework for multimodal sarcasm detection. This work introduces a novel attention mechanism that captures both coarse-grained and fine-grained cross-modal interactions to improve sarcasm detection from text-image pairs.

**Authors**: **Dehao Wu**, Xingjie Zhuang, Haohan Wang
